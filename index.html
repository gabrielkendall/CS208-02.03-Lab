Meridian, Idaho, USA

Location

Between Boise and Nampa longitudinally, and between Kuna and Eagle latitudinally.

About

Weather

Varies often but only rarly goes either below 0 or over 100 degrees. Snow has moved from october to late january over the last 10 years.

Population

As of 2024 the population is estimated to be about 139.74 thousand people.

Fun Facts

  • Meridian has been the fastest growing city in Idaho since 1994, and is in the Top Ten fastest grownign cities in the country.
  • The Idaho State Police is headquartered in Meridian.
  • Meridian was originally settled as Hunter, ID in 1891. Before being renamed to Meridian in 1893.
